How to pronounce my name

Here we use the International Phonetic Alphabet for phonetic transcription, with particular attention to English phonemes.

  • My given name (Amit) is from the Hebrew עמית; it is usually pronounced /ʌˈmit/ in English.
  • My family name (Hazi) is an Arabic demonym حازي which was adopted into Hebrew as חזי; it is usually pronounced [ˈhɑzi] or [ˈhäzi] in English.
